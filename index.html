About Me

Since 2020, I’ve specialized in backend software development, delivering secure, efficient, and scalable systems for diverse industries. My expertise in databases, APIs, and server management ensures that every solution I build improves performance, reliability, and security — key pillars of any successful digital product. My name is Chimeziri Obioha.

Before my tech journey, I was a motorcycle spare parts trader, where I honed my problem-solving skills and developed a strong business acumen. My transition to tech was fueled by a fascination with modern technologies and their potential to transform industries. The challenges I encountered during this shift strengthened my ability to adapt and thrive in fast-paced, evolving environments.

My non-technical background gives me a unique perspective. I approach every project with the same tenacity I had as a trader, paying attention to the smallest details, always striving for excellence because I understand that in business, results matter, and that’s why I ensure that the systems I develop directly contribute to the success of my clients.

Additionally, my strong focus on security also ignited an interest in cybersecurity, and I’m currently taking a course curated by Google cybersecurity experts, while aggressively developing my core backend development skills.
